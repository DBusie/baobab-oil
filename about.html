About Baobab Oil

Jamie baobab oil contains Vitamin A , Vitamin E , Omega fatty acids plus other fatty acids such as linolenic and linoleic acids; the most commonly used fatty acids in skin care products.

  • Fatty acid breakdown:
  • Oleic acid (Omega 9): 23-44%
  • Linoleic acid (Omega 6): 25-37%
  • Linolenic acid (Omega 3): 0.2-3.0%
  • Palmitic acid: 18-30%
  • Stearic acid: 1.5-6.0%

It is an amazing moisturiser that is light weight, smooth textured. It absorbs quickly and is non greasy. Jamie baobab oil promotes rejuvenation of skin cells and wound healing.
