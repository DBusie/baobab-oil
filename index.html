Jamie Baobab Oil

Jamie baobab oil is a nutrient rich oil that is cold pressed from the seeds of the baobab tree. It has a deep golden yellow color and a rich feel. In Zimbabwe, people call the baobab tree “the pharmacy tree” because of its many uses, including uses of baobab oil.
