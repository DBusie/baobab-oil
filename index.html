About Baobab Oil

Baobab oil is a nutrient rich oil that comes from the seeds of the baobab tree. It is obtained by cold pressing the seeds25 and has a deep golden yellow color, a rich feel. it contains vitamin a , vitamin e , omega fatty acids and other fatty acids including linolenic and linoleic acids; the most commonly used fatty acids skin care products. it is an amazing moisturiser that is light weight smooth texture that absorbs quickly and is non greasy In Africa, people call it “the tree of life” and “pharmacy tree” because of its many uses, including for baobab oil. Fatty acid breakdown: Oleic acid (Omega 9): 23-44% Linoleic acid (Omega 6): 25-37% Linolenic acid (Omega 3): 0.2-3.0% Palmitic acid: 18-30% Stearic acid: 1.5-6.0% Promotes rejuvenation of skin cells. Promotes wound healing.
