Jamie Baobab Seed Oil

In Zimbabwe, people call the baobab tree “the pharmacy tree” because of its many uses, including those of baobab oil. Jamie baobab oil is a nutrient rich pure organic oil that is cold pressed from seeds of the baobab tree. It has a deep golden yellow color and a rich feel.
